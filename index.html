About

Hi, I'm Lucy. I am an illustrator, multidisciplinary artist and a small business owner. I am passionate about giving children and adults access to illustrations and artworks that are hand-created - art that is thoughtful, vibrant and full of colours and natural patterns. I live near the ocean and am ever inspired by its vastness and the rhythms of nature. Many of my artworks are an ode to the land: a way to capture a memory of a place and time.

Contact me to enquire about buying prints, commissions or to order custom linocut invitations and cards.
